Fleur de Lis Salon

Fleur de Lis Salon is a family owned hair salon in Port Orange, Florida, proudly serving the community for more than 25 years. For decades, our licensed team has specialized in precision haircuts, expert haircolor, highlights, and professional hair extensions for women, men, and children. We also offer bridal hair and updos, Brazilian Blowout keratin treatments, perms, conditioning services, and facial waxing to give every client the complete salon experience.

At Fleur de Lis Salon, we believe in creating more than just great hair. We’ve built a comfortable, safe space where clients can relax, feel at ease, and receive the personalized attention they deserve. From the moment you walk in, our team listens to your needs and works with you to achieve the look you’re envisioning. Geri Buckley Owner
